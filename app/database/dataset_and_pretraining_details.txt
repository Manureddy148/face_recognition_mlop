# Dataset Details

*   **Dataset Name:** Indian Movie Faces Dataset (IMFDB)
*   **Kaggle URL:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/anirudhsimhachalam/indian-movie-faces-datasetimfdb-face-recognition
*   **Number of Classes:** 100
*   **Number of Images:** 34,512

# Pre-training Details

*   **Model Architecture:** VGG16 with a custom classifier head.
    *   VGG16 (ImageNet weights, frozen base)
    *   GlobalAveragePooling2D
    *   Dense(512, ReLU) + BatchNorm + Dropout(0.5)
    *   Dense(256, ReLU) + BatchNorm + Dropout(0.3)
    *   Dense(num_classes, Softmax)
*   **Image Size:** 224x224
*   **Batch Size:** 16
*   **Epochs:** 30 (default, can be changed with `PRETRAIN_EPOCHS` environment variable)
*   **Optimizer:** Adam (learning_rate=1e-3)
*   **Loss Function:** Categorical Cross-entropy
